Welcome to the Cal Poly Chinese Cultural Club!
This is the place where Chinese people and people who are interested in the Chinese club meet!
We do all kind of fun stuff ranging from CCC bondfire to understanding culture in different regions of China during regular meetings.
Go to our meetings if you are interested! You get to visit and learn more about China.
Most importantly, you get to enjoy some good food! So come join us!
